About Us

X and O (TicTacToe Game) is a modern twist on the classic 3×3 grid challenge — a game of focus, logic, and bragging rights. Designed for both casual and competitive players, it brings a clean, minimal feel mixed with online power-ups, tournaments, and friendly duels. Whether you’re going head-to-head with your buddy, testing your skills against AI, or competing in global tournaments, X and O turns a simple board into a battlefield of wits.
